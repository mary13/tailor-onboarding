Bespoke Dress Tailor

Onboarding Flow Design Experiment

An exploration of onboarding flows for an online bespoke dress shop, designed using Pair Design methodology (Noessel & Anderson). Each scenario explores different customer personas and their unique needs.

Read Documentation View on GitHub

Scenarios

Scenario A: Event-Driven First-Timer

✓ Complete - 16 screens

Sarah needs a custom dress for a gala in 10 weeks. She's browsing the catalog and ready to order.

Key tension: Urgency (deadline) vs. Confidence (measurements, fabric choice)

Paths: Swatch detour (10 screens) • Order now (6 screens)
